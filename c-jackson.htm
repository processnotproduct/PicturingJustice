Chris Jackson

 

 Biography:

Chris Jackson teaches writing, literature, and music history at Nova Southeastern University in Ft. Lauderdale. She is completing Night
Sirens: Myth and Ritual in Contemporary Women's Mysteries (McFarland Publishing). She has a Ph.D. in American Literature.Chris savors the mystery in life. She runs Mystery Lovers discussion group at Barnes & Noble in Plantation, Florida. She is co-chair of
Sleuthfest 2001, the annual conference for Mystery Writers of America. "Mystery
on Miami Beach Bookstore" newsletter and Fort Lauderdale's Sun-Sentinel have published her hard copy book reviews. In addition to commentary in Picturing Justice, her mystery reviews appear in Orchard Press Mysteries,
an online journal. www.orchardpressmysteries.com

Mystery fiction is another writing outlet. Chris seeks an agent/publisher for Last Resort Investigations mystery novels featuring Andie Knight, a woman PI. "Clean Up," a short story in Orchard Press (March 2000), shows Andie Knight in action.
