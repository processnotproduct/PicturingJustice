John Denvir

 Biography:

John Denvir is a Professor at the University of San Francisco School of Law where he teaches Constitutional and  Admininstrative Law.  He has written extensively on constitutional and legal theory, focussing on the insights which can be drawn from the study of literature and film. He is editor of Legal Reelism: Movies as Legal Texts ( U. Ill. Press 1996).
