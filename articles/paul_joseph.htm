Paul Joseph

 

 Biography:

Webitor Paul Joseph - Associate Dean for International and External programs -  teaches constitutional law, torts, and criminal procedure at Nova Southeastern University in Fort Lauderdale, Florida.   He is co-editor of Prime Time Law: Television as Legal Narrative (Carolina Academic Press, 1998), a book of essays on the interactions between law and television.
