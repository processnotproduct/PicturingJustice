The Devil's Advocate

by Robert L. Waring

This section reveals more about the film Devil’s Advocate than someone who has not seen it would want to know. If you read on, it will spoil some of the film’s surprises.

Wasn’t the film a perverse spin on the Star Wars trilogy plot? Son discovers evil man is actually his father and that a beautiful woman in his life is actually his sister. The twist here is that dad wants the son to have sex with the sister while he watches. Darth Vader was never that evil, but then, he wasn’t a lawyer.

This film is certain to inspire more lawyer jokes. Example: What is the real reason many Americans are reluctant to support gun control? Answer: Fewer lawyers would commit suicide.
